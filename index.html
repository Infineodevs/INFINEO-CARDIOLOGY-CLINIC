Cardiac Excellence

🩺

Our Services

Comprehensive Cardiac Care

From routine checkups to complex cardiac evaluations, we offer a full spectrum of heart health services.

❤️

Heart Checkup

Comprehensive cardiac examination and evaluation to assess your overall heart health and detect early warning signs.

📊

ECG

Electrocardiogram recording to detect arrhythmias, coronary artery disease, and other cardiac conditions accurately.

🩸

Blood Pressure Monitoring

Advanced BP monitoring and hypertension management to keep your cardiovascular system in optimal condition.

🩺

Cardiac Consultation

In-depth one-on-one consultations with our specialist cardiologist for diagnosis, treatment planning, and guidance.

🛡️

Preventive Heart Care

Personalised preventive strategies, lifestyle counselling, and risk factor management for long-term heart health.
